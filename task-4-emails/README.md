## 3. Social Media Security: My Personal Checklist

| What I Should Do (Do's) | What I Should Avoid (Don'ts) |
| :--- | :--- |
| Set up 2-Step Verification on WhatsApp and Instagram. | Never share your OTP or login codes with anyone. |
| Regularly check my privacy settings to see who can view my posts. | Don't click on "Win a Gift" or random prize links in DMs. |
| Use a strong password that is hard to guess. | Stop adding people I don't know in real life. |
| Always log out from public computers (like in a lab). | Don't share my phone number or address in public comments. |

**Do’s (Responsible Practices)**

**\* Think before you post — ensure your content is respectful and appropriate**

**\* Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication**

**\* Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation**

**\* Respect others’ privacy — ask permission before posting their photos or details**

**\* Maintain a professional tone, especially on platforms like LinkedIn**

**\* Report suspicious accounts or harmful content**

**\* Be mindful of your digital footprint (what you post stays online)**

**Don’ts (Things to Avoid)**

**\* Don’t share personal or sensitive information (address, phone number, OTPs)**

**\* Don’t engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or hate speech**

**\* Don’t accept friend requests from unknown or suspicious profiles**

**\* Don’t spread rumors, fake news, or unverified content**

**\* Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content**

**\* Don’t overshare your location in real time**

**\* Don’t click on unknown or suspicious links**

Part B: Stay Safe Online - Prevention Checklist
Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money. PIN is only for sending money.

Don't click on links sent by strangers via WhatsApp or SMS.

Keep a separate, strong password for your phone and your banking apps.

(Financial Safety): Always check the name of the person appearing on the UPI app before confirming a payment.

(Financial Safety): Never share your OTP or bank login details with anyone on a call.

Set a daily transaction limit on your UPI app to minimize risk.

Regularly update your banking apps to get the latest security features.

Don't use public Wi-Fi for doing any money transactions.

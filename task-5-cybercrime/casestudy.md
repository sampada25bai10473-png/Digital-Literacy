Part A: Case Study - UPI Payment Fraud
What the crime is: UPI fraud is a trick where scammers use fake QR codes or "Request Money" links to steal money from people's bank accounts using apps like GPay, PhonePe, or Paytm.

How it happens (Step-by-Step):

The scammer finds a target on a marketplace or through social media and shows interest in buying/selling something.

They send a QR code or a "Request Money" notification to the victim.

They tell the victim that scanning the code or entering the UPI PIN is necessary to receive the payment.

As soon as the victim enters the PIN, money is deducted from their account instantly.

Target Group: Mostly students, new online sellers, and people who aren't very familiar with how UPI requests work.

Consequences: Loss of hard-earned money and potential access to other linked bank details.
